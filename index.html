Amsterdam

If you never have visited Amsterdam you would be amazed by the many culture influences the city has to offer. When visiting the first thing you would notice is that most cities and neighboring towns are connected with canals. Most would describe Amsterdam as efficient and quaint with gingerbread style homes. Young and old mostly travel by bicycles or scooters all year round due to it one-way streets system. At a local bike shop you can rent a bicycle for a under 12 euro’s day. With an OV-chip card you travel on all public transports sponsored by NS, GVB, Connexxion and RET. I am Amsterdam card is a city pass when keeping up on local deals.

Reminder if you want to make use of public transport it is possible to buy a ticket by pin on the bus, tram or at the stops, due to new regulations. If you only have cash at hand you can buy public transportation tickets at local post offices or at metro/train stations. When exploring the Dutch history it starts at Amsterdam Central station. Back in old ages the dutch were know for there transport by ships. That is were the tradition eating haring with unions a delicacy come from. Tulpen and klompen are known souvenirs. When it comes to baked goods and sweet treats tradition starts at stroopwafel, bitterballen and poffertjes and. At the Heineken Experience you can explore premium beer brand culture.

Bos en Lommer

De Reinhardt vos straat is were bos en lommer gets its name from. Before the second world war this area had a petting zoo called bos en lommer. With bos referencing the many trees and Lommer the leaves.

XO Blue Square and Infinity hotels are located at bos en lommer. It is 1min in walking distance from the local market and public transportation stops. Our 175 rooms are a smoke free environment divided over three storey’s to make your stay as safe and convenient as possible. Each room is provided with a flat screen tv, safe, air conditioning, refrigerator, phone, heating, flatiron & board, Nespresso machine and of course free WIFI. At reservations you can choose what kind of bathroom you like with complimentary shower or bathing supplies or utilities. If needed you can extend your stay buy clicking on the link or reach us at phone number 0202629263.

To start your day right it is important to have a good breakfast. Our buffet is accessible from Monday to Friday from 7am till 10:30 and on weekends from 7.30 till 11 am. Per adult its 15 euro’s and kids between 4 and 11 years old it is only 7,50 euro. If have allergies or special needs please let us know at reservations.

Erasmus park

The Erasmus park is situated in the middle of a residential area that is divided in three main fields. On the first fields soccer is usually played. On the second field events take place when the weather is nice people usually go here to organize a barbeque to get away from the city. On the last field a playground can be found for children.

Simone’s Paella Cooking

At hoofdweg 923 you can find Simone’s Paella Cooking Class. Here you can explore the wonderful cousin of the Spanish table. The cooking courses are at a reasonable price with sessions up to an hour or two.

Podium mozaik

Podium mozaik is a theare and café / restaurant it is a meeting place for different culture. The program consists of music, comedy, spoken word, storytelling, dance and crossover from local and international. In the café/restaurant you can have breakfast, lunch and dinner for a small fee. Every weekend traditional Turkish cousine is served
